Cookies

The author of this page does not track his visitors on his own. He also does not use any social buttons. That said, this page is hosted on GitHub which may use non-essential cookies, though it seems not to, and, in fact, I haven’t found any as yet. Also, the site uses Google fonts and, on some posts, several reputable javascript libraries like MathJax and Mermaidjs hosted not locally but on CDN’s. In fact, I found some mysterious cookie called CONSENT from gstatic.com, which, apparently, is used to manage consent on google services. Hence, be aware that this site may contain cookies, and also other tracking devices employed by seemingly benign external resources.
